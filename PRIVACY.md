# FREEDOM Coin Privacy Policy

## What We Collect
FREEDOM Coin is fully decentralised.
We collect nothing. There are no servers.
Your data stays on your device only.

## Your Wallet
Your private keys never leave your device.
Your transactions are quantum encrypted.
Nobody including the creator can access your wallet.

## Blockchain Data
All transaction data on the blockchain is encrypted.
Stealth transactions are completely invisible.
Only sender and receiver can see transaction details.

## Your Identity
Your biological key generation data never leaves your device.
Your FREEDOM ID is stored locally only.
No government or company can access your identity.
